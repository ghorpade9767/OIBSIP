Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About Him

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India and one of the most respected scientists in the country. He played a key role in India's missile development programs and earned the title "Missile Man of India."

He was known for his humility, dedication, and love for students. He inspired millions of young people to dream big and work hard to achieve success.

Why I Admire Him

I admire Dr. Kalam for his simplicity and dedication to the nation. Despite achieving great success, he remained humble and always motivated students to focus on education and innovation.
